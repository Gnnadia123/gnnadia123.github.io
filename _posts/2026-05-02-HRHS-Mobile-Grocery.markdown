On 2nd May 2026, I participated in the 好人好事 Hao Ren Hao Shi mobile grocery program at Tampines West Community Club as a volunteer along with my class. My role was a side game usher, responsible for entertaining the elderly while they wait for their name to be called to collect the food. 

Playing flag erasers against the elderly was a fun and eye-opening experience, especially with many of them having lived a fulfilling life. They shared their wisdom and life experiences with us, allowing us to learn more about their generation as well as the lessons they've learnt from the challenges back then. 

![Playing flag erasers with the beneficiaries.](/images/flagErasers-hrhs-2026.jpg)

Thanks to HRHS for giving us this amazing opportunity, Tampines MP Mr Masagos Zulkifli for coming and uplifting our spirits, SST for planning and reaching out for this Values-In-Action program, and all those involved with the planning committee for S3-03 for arranging these activities.
