---
Our S3-03 debate journey was truly one to remember. 

The debate journey was gruelling, hours of preparation leading up to each debate. In the preliminaries, we faced S3-01, opposing the motion "This house believes that more resources should be spent on elderly than on youth development". We fought hard, ultimately winning the debate. Congrats to Esther for winning best speaker! 

This initial win allowed us to progress to the semi-finals, alongside S3-06, S3-08, and S3-10. We were matched against S3-06, proposing the motion "This house would implement a 4-day work week." The topic seemed simple, yet both parties had many points to say about the motion. After a close battle, we narrowly won against S3-06, progressing to the finals of the debate, which would be held in front of the whole S3 cohort. 

The finals on T3W1 Friday against S3-10 were nerve-wracking. The motion was quite political, being "This house would hold social media companies legally liable for the decline in youth mental health". Seeing 200 people seated in front of you while having to manage your own script and engage in POIs (Point Of Information) was a massive task. Both parties fought valiantly, having aggresive strategies where many POIs were raised, attacked, and defended against. Overall, the experience was fun, both classes not letting up until the very end. 

![The scene at the debate finals](/assets/images/me-debate.jpg)

While waiting for the results, we also engaged in a floor debate, opening the motion to members of the floor. This opened our insights to points we could've raised, but never thought of. Ultimately, we managed to win the debate, making us the overall Champions of the S3 Student Congress! I am also incredibly honoured to receive the title of Best Speaker for the debate, being the first Opposition speaker. 

We had lots of fun during the Debate, and S3-10 put up a seriously strong fight. The results were marginally close, and was a true experience ingrained into my brain. It was a tough battle, and one that many agreed was a heated and exciting one.
