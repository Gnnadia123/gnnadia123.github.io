# SSTride Experience #
It's a wrap! SSTride for wellness is officially over, along with Growcalth Season 3! 

Being a Sports and Wellness Representative for Black House, I led the Secondary Ones, along with my House Excos, from Rifle Range Park all the way back to SST. At first, morale was low, especially with us going last after the initial flag-off minigames. However, after walking for a while, and completing the first game station, people started opening up to each other - from complete strangers to close friends. It was a sight to behold as they bonded as one house. 

![Gathering at Rifle Range](/images/sstride-gathering.jpeg)

At Greenleaf View, we managed to overtake Blue House, who had stopped for a break. While it wasn't a race, and no extra points were awarded for getting back first, the friendly competition sparked a morale boost, encouraging us to do better. After a few wrong turns and coincidentally meeting with the Secondary 4 Blue House group at our route junction, we arrived at Buona Vista, for our second game station.

When we finally arrived at school, everyone was exhausted, but you could tell they had fun. As a Sports and Wellness Rep, I was glad to be able to carry out this responsibility successfully with no injuries or problems along the way. The cherry on top - For S1 route minigames, Black house won champion! It was a significant contribution to our overall SSTride ranking, which saw us come in 2nd overall. 

## Growcalth ##

![Final Growcalth Standings](/images/growcalth-26.jpg)

With SSTride ending, the Growcalth season had also ended, with Black House having an amazing comeback story from 3rd to 1st, and comfortably dominating the leaderboard in the last week with over a 600 point lead. Before we set off on SSTride, Black House Captain Yuning got everyone to do their daily challenges, propelling us further into the lead and securing the victory with ease.
